<p>Business Application Programming Interfaces (<strong>BAPIs</strong>) are one of the most powerful tools in SAP, designed to enable seamless integration between SAP and external systems. In most cases, the <strong>standard BAPIs</strong> provided by SAP are sufficient to cover typical business scenarios.</p><p>However, real-world requirements often demand more. Maybe you need an extra field that isn’t delivered, or you have to add custom checks before the data is posted. That’s when <strong>enhancing a standard BAPI</strong> becomes necessary.</p>
